  Food Bank   February 02, 2020
Food help fuelled by Donation

Cuaderno have opened their wallets and pantries to help people struggling at this corona period. It was a whole Kerala food Drive that began Aug 2nd and ended Wednesday. Students donated 2706 cans and dried foods for the food bank surpassing their goal of 2500.
